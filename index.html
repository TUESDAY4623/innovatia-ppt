Know the Difference

Vibe Coding vs Self-Automated

🎵

Vibe Coding

AI-assisted creativity
  • You describe what you want in natural language
  • AI generates code, you refine iteratively
  • Fast prototyping — ideas to demo in minutes
  • Focus is on speed and exploration
  • Great for early-stage brainstorming
  • Human acts as a director, AI as the builder
VS
⚙️

Self-Automated Project

System-driven execution
  • Pre-defined logic and rules built into the system
  • Code runs autonomously without human prompting
  • Scalable and repeatable — runs consistently
  • Focus is on reliability and production
  • Great for deployed, real-world solutions
  • System acts as the engine, human as overseer

Round 1 of 3
ROUND 1

Idea Presentation

What happens here?

  • Present your socially relevant business idea
  • Explain the problem you're solving
  • Show your proposed solution
  • Keep it crisp — 3 to 5 minutes per team
  • Be ready for judge Q&A

💡 Tips to Crack Round 1

  • Know your target audience clearly
  • Show real data or examples of the problem
  • Keep it simple — don't overcomplicate
  • Practice your pitch at least twice before coming
Round 2 of 3
ROUND 2

AI Solution Creation

What happens here?

  • Build an AI-powered prototype of your idea
  • Use available AI tools & platforms in the lab
  • Work in computer labs with internet access
  • Time-bound creation window — work fast & smart
  • This is where ideas become REAL

🛠 Tips to Crack Round 2

  • Focus on a working demo, not perfection
  • Divide tasks within your team efficiently
  • Use AI tools like ChatGPT, Canva AI, GitHub Copilot
  • Have a backup plan if something breaks
Round 3 of 3
ROUND 3

Final Pitching

What happens here?

  • Present your complete idea + AI prototype
  • Show the full pipeline: Problem → AI Solution → Impact
  • Demonstrate your working prototype live
  • Defend your approach in front of judges

🎤 Tips to Crack Round 3

  • Start with a powerful hook — grab attention in 10 seconds
  • Show the prototype FIRST, then explain
  • Be confident when answering questions
  • End with a strong closing statement
